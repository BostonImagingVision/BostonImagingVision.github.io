Running events costs money, and we ask for help in helping bringing our community together. Our biggest expense is paying for pizza.

One Year Membership

Organization are invited to become a member of Boston Imaging and Vision. For $1500:
  • Your logo is displayed prominantly on our home page, and at the beginning of every meetup.
  • Your are invited to bring a technical speaker to our events and present.
  • List up to two job listings in our e-mails for up to 2 months.
  • The organizers of Boston Imaging and Vision will attempt to make introductions to people who can help you reach your recruiting and algorithmic goals.

Other Sponsorship

  • You may sponsor a particular meetup for $500. For that evening, you recieve a speaking position on a topic relevant to the community. Or, you can get up and talk for a few minutes. We also list up to two job listings in our e-mail for 2 months.
  • We ask for $25 to list jobs in e-mail for one month.
